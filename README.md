## Hi there, I'm Yikun Zhang (张奕堃) 👋

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the [Department of Statistics](https://stat.uw.edu/) at the **University of Washington, Seattle**, advised by [Prof. Yen-Chi Chen](http://faculty.washington.edu/yenchic/). Previously, I obtained my M.S. in Statistics at UW (2020) and my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics at Sun Yat-Sen University (2018).

---

### 🔬 Research Interests

- **Nonparametric Statistics** (Kernel Smoothing, Density Estimation)
- **Optimization on Nonlinear Manifolds** (Mean Shift Algorithms, Mode Clustering)
- **High-Dimensional Inference** with Missing/Incomplete Data
- **Causal Inference** for Continuous Treatments (Dose-Response Curves)
- **Transfer Learning** and Domain Adaptation
- **Computational Astronomy** (Cosmic Web Detection and Analysis)
